About Us

Mission: To create a thriving community around delicious, burger plantain skin patties. We believe in the power of healthy eating to enhance well-being, and we're committed to making nutritious and satisfying food accessible to everyone.


Vision: To create a sustainable and healthy plant-based burger made with banana skin and plantain, while promoting environmental responsibility and reducing food waste.


Our Story: What began as a school idea quickly turned into a flavorful movement. We saw waste in banana peels and turned it into wonder. We hope to show the world that healthy and eco-friendly food can still taste amazing!
